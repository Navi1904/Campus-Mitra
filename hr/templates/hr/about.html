Jawaharlal Nehru College of Engineering and Technology

Jawaharlal Nehru College of Engineering and Technology (JNCET) is a premier institution offering quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Established in 1985, JNCET has been at the forefront of technical education, providing students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their careers.

Our mission is to foster innovation, research, and excellence in engineering education. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and more.

At JNCET, we are committed to creating a vibrant learning environment that encourages students to achieve their full potential. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations ensure that our students receive a holistic education that prepares them for the challenges of the modern world.
